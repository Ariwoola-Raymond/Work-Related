  • If customer's Dnata Travel Voucher is expired
  • If customer is unable to redeem their travel voucher at Dnata outlets

 

Noon Voucher - Duo Card 

Check Points

  • Customer Met AED 5000 spent amount after 30days
  • Customer Signed application form April 2022 however card onboarded / creation date is for May

Always On Offer 

Check Points

  • Lead created after card open date
  • No Lead created
  • This is existing customer
  • Customer didn't make the min spend of AED 1 within first billing 

Marriott Bonvoy Card  

Check Points

Welcome Bonus 

  • Customer didn't receive Welcome bonus points since he didn't pay Joining fee as per 1st billing 
  • No Joining fee billed to the card 
  • Customer requesting to transfer bonus points before the tat of 4-6 weeks
  • Joining fee billed however it got reversed , customer requesting for acquisition offer to be given as exception 

 

Spends Bonus
  • Customer met the spent criteria after 3 billing
  • No Joining fee billed to the card and customer wants the spend bonus
  • Since card issued FYF customer requesting to charge Joining fee and qualify for welcome + spend bonus
  • Customer applied for AED 100,000k Welcome & spend bonus however, since card onboarded in the different month hence received lower bonus points

 

For the below cards same check points applicable as Marriott Bonvoy Card.   

  • Skywards Infinite / Signature
  • Etihad Elevate / Inspire
  • U by Emaar Infinite / Signature
  • Dnata World / Platinum Card
  • Business Rewards Signature  

Skywards Infinite 

Check point 

  • Customer requesting for the Silver Tier upgrade & Rotana Club membership before paying the Joining fee  

Lulu Platinum / Titanium 

Check Points 

  • Customer paid Joining fee in 2nd, or 3rd billing instead of 1st and requesting for 250 lulu points
  • Customer met the spent criteria in the 3rd or 4th billing instead of 2nd and requesting for bonus points of 250 to be given as exception

Manchester United Credit Card 

Check Points

Home Jersey -

  1. Customer met the spent threshold of AED 2,000 in 2nd month instead of first
  2. Spend AED 2,000 within 30 days of Activation of card
  • If the customer hasn't received the Jersey, offer AED 300 as Cashback (into current account or CC cashback)
  • If customer insists on the jersey, it is mandatory to confirm the jersey size and check with TL if the size is available
    • If available, the delivery should be done within 7 working days
    • If not available, inform the customer the same and re-offer AED 300 Cashback
      • If the customer insists on the jersey, raise the exception SR

Red Box -

  1. Customer met the spent threshold of AED 6,000 in 4th month instead of 3rd

  2. Spend AED 6,000 in 3 months (Automation process through Aramex)

  • Customer should receive the Red Box by the 4th month
    • Debit / Credit Card - Based on card activation
      • If the TAT has been breached, raise the exception SR

Complaints related to Credit Card Acquisition Offers

If the customer is complaining about the following;

  • Not receiving the Joining bonus for EK/Marriott 
  • Not receiving Free Night Offer on card renewal after AMF is paid on Marriott Bonvoy cards
    • In such cases, check on the statement that 1 year has passed and that customer has paid the AMF
    • Ensure to capture the customer eligibility on the SR notes (if eligible or if not eligible and customer insists on having the complaint raised)
  • Not receiving vouchers upon joining
  • Any other credit card bonus related to the Acquisition Offer

Important checks

  • Card needs to be active
  • Check creation date & Map it with Acquisition offers

Kindly note for Credit Card applications through website and paid channels (Facebook, Instagram, Google Search, Google Discovery Ads, Mobile Banking, Online Banking), a lead must have been created before the card issuance date to be eligible for the offer. If there is no lead created on the CIF or in their alternative CIF, DO NOT raise a Credit Card promotion complaint SR for this case as the customer did not meet the eligibility criteria.

 

  • Check the TAT upon making the payment for the joining fees / the total spend in 3 billing cycles for the complementary points/miles for Marriott card or Skywards
