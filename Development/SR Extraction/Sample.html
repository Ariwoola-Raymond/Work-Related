ENBD X Troubleshooting - Registration and Reset Issues

Summary

This article highlights ENBD X troubleshooting and complaints for registration and migration issues and is intended to guide agents on how to assist customers with troubleshooting their issues and raising complaints as required.

Key points and pre-requisites 

Before assisting customers with ENBD X Registration / Reset troubleshooting, ensure that the customer has the latest ENBD X version for both iOS and Android which can be checked on their respective App stores and should always be as per latest version released by the bank. Do the below health checks first with the customer:

Always check first under Interactions - Online & Mobile tab, Customer Status and accordingly guide customer with the registration issue. Click here for more details.

  • Close all apps and launch ENBD X
  • Disables/disconnects any VPN on their device prior to opening ENBD X or using any service on their mobile device
  • Check the Network connectivity – using strong WiFi, not restricted such as corporate or public WiFi, alternatively to use their mobile data
  • Make sure that the customer's mobile phone operating system (Android or iOS) is compatible with the latest version of ENBD X
    • Android – version 9 and upwards
    • Apple – iOS 15.0 and upwards
  • Date of birth should be matching in CRM details – dd/mm/yyyy format
  • Check that the customer is using an active account/card and that the CIF not suspended or blacklisted
  • Jointly owned/operated accounts will not be able to register for Online Banking

Once the above checks are done, proceed with assisting the customer.

For the below error messages, assist the customer accordingly:

  • "User is already registered or you do not have the privileges to register directly from this app." - Check the registration status and assist the customer accordingly
  • "Sorry, we are not able to log you in because you have entered invalid credentials or you are not eligible to use this application." - Assist the customer with the Reset process

Registration issues and troubleshooting

Check channel footprints and customer status in Interactions on CRM

Ensure to check the Channel Footprints on CRM and read the description clearly to understand the reason of the error before raising any SR.

Check in Interactions (Sample screenshot) - Online & Mobile tab if Customer status shows as:

  • Active – assist customer with Forgot credentials troubleshooting steps
    • For Password reset, click here
    • For Retrieving user ID, click here
  • Reg_init – Incomplete registration process. Guide the customer through the registration process steps
  • Blank – Guide the customer through the registration process steps

View the customer's ENBD X registration/reset activity details through CRM channel footprints (Sample screenshots)

Troubleshooting

 Ensure to do all the required troubleshooting prior to raising the complaint SR.

Email verification code not received

  • If the customer has a company email address and is not receiving the email verification, please advise them to change the email to a personal one (ie. Hotmail, Gmail, etc.). 
    • In case the customer is not willing to change the email address, inform them to check with their email service provider if there is any block placed.

Confirm with customer if the registered email address appearing for Registration matches with bank records

  • If the email is matching
    • Ask the customer to check other folders (junk/spam) in their email
    • Check email directly from the email address folders by minimizing the ENBD X screen, then go to email folders in the device
    • Ask the customer if there are any restrictions/blocks/rules on their email while receiving bulk messages which may cause the issue
    • Alternatively, ask the customer to update their email with a different email address
  • If email is not matching, ask customer to update their registered email address and try again

If email is still not received:

  • Ask the customer to check their respective email provider settings to ensure that there are no restrictions placed for Emirates NBD
  • Advise the customer to retry the process and upon reaching the “Verify email” stage again, stay on that same page
  • Then through a laptop/desktop, login to the email domain registered with the bank (ex. Yahoo or Gmail), then click the ‘Verify email’ link
  • The customer will then have to look back at ENBD X app where OTP SMS page will appear, and push OTP SMS will be sent

If the customer is still facing the issue after the above troubleshooting, raise the below request.

  • Ensure to capture the customer's mobile device details

Verification Failed

Ensure to do the below:

  • If verification fails, advise the customer to tap the back arrow on the top left hand side of the screen to return back to the verify email page where it should automatically push to the "success" screen, after which the OTP SMS page will appear, and push OTP will be sent

OTP SMS not received

If a customer calls due to not receiving an OTP, check the following:

  • If the customer is receiving other SMSs from Emirates NBD
  • If the customer is using a dual sim
  • If the customer is roaming 
  • If the customer's registered mobile number is matching the number they are using
  • Check if registered mobile number is updated as “preferred” in our system. A “check mark” should be seen (Sample screenshot). If not, then raise the below SR:
Type Area Sub Area TAT
Complaint Account Account maintenance issues 5 working days

If all the above checks are done and the issue persists, request the customer to re-initiate the service and to tap "Get Call Back" to receive their OTP via IVR.

SMS OTP – IVR call back (POM call process) has been introduced for customers who reports issue for SMS OTP not received. This solution should be offered as part of troubleshooting complaints pertaining to any of below 6 services and is applicable for both UAE and International mobile numbers.

RMA – Retail Mobile Application 

ROA – Retail Online Application

  • Registration - RMA and ROA
  • Smart Pass Activation - RMA
  • Reactivation of Smart Pass - RMA
  • Forgot password - RMA and ROA
  • Forgot username - RMA and ROA
  • Online banking MFA login – ROA

Please advise customers that while receiving the POM call from the bank's registered mobile number (02 - 301 2222) to authorise the reset process using untrusted devices or Online Banking, they need to listen to the complete recorded message before pressing any buttons:

  • Press 3 to authorise the request
  • Press 9 if the request is not authorised and should be reported as "Fraud"
  • Press 1 to listen to the menu again

Reset issues and troubleshooting

It is mandatory to ask the customer at which stage the failure is happening and the source on which the customer is trying to register on (Mobile or Online Banking), and to mention the same in the SR notes.

Check under Interactions > Online & Mobile tab if there are any recent logins seen and what is the error message displayed (Sample screenshots) . If none, assist the customer based on the mentioned Customer Status as follows:

Active

If status is showing as “Active” but the customer is unable to reset/login, assist customer with Forgot credentials troubleshooting steps:

  • For Password reset, click here
  • For Retrieving user ID, click here

It is mandatory to ask customers to do Forgot password first, then to do Forgot username. However, after multiple attempts of resetting it will block the access to login, but status will remain Active on CRM. In such cases, inform the customer to wait for one hour and try forgot credentials once again in the same order.

BLOCKED-INVALID-OTP-ATTEMPTS 

This status means that customers have entered an invalid OTP SMS multiple times while resetting any of their credentials. Guide the customer to do the steps again. For example:

  • If the customer was trying to retrieve their user ID and access got blocked, advise the customer to Reset Password then if required Retrieve User ID .

If reset attempts have been exceeded, ask the customer to wait for one hour before trying again.

Blocked by customer

This status means that the profile was blocked by CCO via an SR. Check the SR list to validate the same.

If the customer confirms that no fraud has happened, raise the unblock SR below then proceed to guide the customer to Reset Password and Retrieve User ID if required:

Type Area Sub Area TAT
Service Online Mobile Banking Online banking profile unblock Instant

Blocked by bank

This status means there was a bank initiated block on the customer's profile. 

Check CRM pop-up / SR raised on the customer's profile to identify the nature of the block. For more information, click here.

Blocked by Bank - HRAR

For customers that are unable to login to the ENBD X Mobile/ Online Banking due to the below error message, ensure to follow the below steps:

“Your account has been temporarily blocked due to pending profile update. kindly check your registered email inbox and reply to: Kycprofileupdate@emiratesnbd.com to resolve the issue. For any further assistance, feel free to contact us”

  • Check customers CIF on CRM and see if they received the following pop-up message alert - click here
  • Check CRM notes on the customers profile to see if the HRAR block is placed
  • Refer to the PEGA tool for document information and follow the guidelines mentioned
    • For more information on the PEGA tool - click here
    • If you are unable to access PEGA, inform the customer that they will receive a KYC Update Notification email from info@emiratesnbd.com
    • Advice customer to upload the required documents via website (How to Update KYC | KYC Details Update Support | Emirates NBD) OR they can send an email to kycprofileupdate@emiratesnbd.com
  • Inform the customer that the request will be processed within 10 working days, once they submit all the required documents for the account restrictions to be removed
    • If the TAT has exceeded 10 days and the account is still blocked, send an email to your/ available Team Leader (TL)
      • TL will then need to send an email to CDDOpsHelpDesk2@EmiratesNBD.com for further inquiry

Blocked_CDD

How to check if limit restriction is removed/CDD is completed:

  • Go to Customer 360 page
  • On the ribbon, click the drop down icon and select Interactions
  • Under Interactions, select Enrolment to view the delivery and CDD completion status

If the issue is DAO limit related:

  • Check transaction limits on CRM (Should not be Zero)
    • Search with customer CIF - go to “Interactions” - select “Online & Mobile” tab (trusted device status) & “Enrolment” tab (CDD status & Delivery status)

If the issue is related to Debit Card delivery or profile update:

  • Confirm if the welcome pack is delivered - Debit Card is delivered and activated.
  • Confirm if the customer logged in on ENBD X and updated their profile through the blue banner on the home screen.
  • If the above steps are followed, the account limit restrictions will be lifted, and the account will be fully operational.

Fraud block

This status happens when the customer pushes 9 instead of 3 during the POM call while resetting their credentials from an untrusted device or Online Banking. 

Probe that the same is not a fraud scenario by following the steps under Retrieve/change credentials for Online Banking or untrusted mobile device to unblock the customer's profile.

Once done, and profile is unblocked, the status will change to "Unblock Password Change"

Unblock password change

This status means that the customer had their profile unblocked but have not yet done a password reset. In this case, guide the customer to Reset Password.

Registration steps

Follow the below steps for ENBD X Registration:

  • Select Register now
  • Enter active account or card number and date of birth
    • Date of birth should be marching in CRM details - dd/mm/yyyy format
    • Ensure the account/card status entered are both "Active"
    • Ask the customer to read out the EIDA number and match it with the details showing in CRM
    • Check the Mode of Operations (Sample screenshot) as it should not be blank or 99999. If so, raise the below SR:
Type Area Sub Area TAT
Complaint Account Account maintenance issues 5 working days
  • The customer will then have to click on “Verify email” where it will take them to their registered email domain with the bank (if same is available on the same device), where the “Verify email” link will be provided
    • In case email is not received, click here
    • In case Verification has failed, click here
  • Enter the OTP SMS received on their registered mobile number with the bank
    • In case OTP is not received, click here
  • Create a strong password
    • Upper and lower case
    • Special characters allowed: @, $, ^, #, !, %, *, ?
    • At least 1 number
    • At least 8 characters long
  • The customer will need to activate their Smart Pass
    • In case of any issues with Smart Pass activation, click here
  • After successful activation, the customer will need to make their device trusted
    • In case of any issues with making their device trusted, click here

Do not raise this complaint SR at the start of the call, only after all troubleshooting steps are exhausted based on the customer's issue.

  • Mention the exact troubleshooting done on the call (do not mention "All troubleshooting is done")
  • Clear notes explaining the customer's issue
    • If the customer is facing issues to register, mention why
    • If the customer insists on raising the complain, mention why
